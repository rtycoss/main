Opportunities

Prospective DPhil/PhD candidates who meet the admissions eligibility criteria are most welcome to apply to join the group. Students taking the MSc, MCompSci and related courses can work with us by choosing or proposing a project in the topics above. Regrettably we cannot accommodate internships.

Our main clinical collaborators specialize in orthopaedics, which constitutes our primary data source. Proposals for research projects for a different clinical field are gladly received, but please state your expected data provenance, or how we can work together to secure an appropriate dataset.
